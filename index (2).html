Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know

Is my photo stored on your servers? ▼
No. Your photos are processed and immediately deleted. We never store, share, or use your personal photos. Your memories are private and safe.
What types of photos work best? ▼
MemoryHD works on all types — old scanned photos, blurry camera shots, faded prints, damaged pictures, and even black & white photos. The worse the original, the more dramatic the improvement!
How do I pay? Is it safe? ▼
We use Paddle, a globally trusted payment processor. You can pay with any Visa or Mastercard. All transactions are encrypted and secure. We never see your card details.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼
Yes, absolutely. Cancel anytime from your account with one click. No questions asked, no hidden fees. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
What is the maximum output quality? ▼
Premium users get output up to 8K resolution (7680×4320 pixels) — sharp enough to print on a large canvas or billboard. Free users get full HD (1080p) output.
